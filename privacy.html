2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the App and to user information handled in connection with the App.

3. Information Collected

3-1. Text Entered by the User

The App formats text entered or pasted by the user on the user's device.

The Developer does not collect, store, upload, or receive text entered or pasted into the App.

3-2. Information Stored on the Device

The App does not store entered text, formatted text, history data, or account information on the device.

3-3. Automatically Collected Information

The App does not automatically collect user information.

3-4. Personal or Sensitive Information

The App does not collect or share personal information or sensitive user data.

3-5. External Services and Third-Party SDKs

The App provides a function to open legal pages in an external browser.

  • Package used: url_launcher
  • Purpose: to open legal pages in an external browser

After opening a legal page in an external browser, the handling of information is governed by the user's browser, network environment, and the destination website.

Because the App does not collect text entered or pasted by the user, the Developer does not use that text.

If the user contacts the Developer by email, the Developer may use information provided by the user for support, identity confirmation when necessary, defect investigation, quality improvement, and legal compliance.

5. Sharing and Third-Party Provision

The Developer does not provide personal information to third parties without the user's consent, except where required by law.

6. Data Storage

The App handles data as follows.

  • Entered text: not stored
  • Formatted text: not stored
  • History data: not stored
  • Account information: no account is created
  • Server transmission: none
  • Cloud sync: none
  • External service storage: none

"Offline" means that the core text formatting process does not require network communication. A web page may be opened in an external browser only when viewing legal information.
